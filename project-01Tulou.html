Healing the Tulou

2021-2022 Individual academic project Location:Zhangzhou City, Fujian Province, China.
Tulou is a traditional residential building in Fujian, China. Its long history, unique culture and circular architectural form attract countless tourists to visit. Out of my interest in Tulou’s prototype and situation, I chose a Tulou in Tianluokeng village as my project site for reutilization. My strategy is to preserve the soil wall and the shape of Tulou while redesigning the structure and the inner space function. The redesigned Tulou as a complex combined with attraction and residence; on the one hand, it will provide better living conditions for the aborigines; on the other hand, newly activated public space functions such as homestay and exhibition will be a resource for locals to make their living, promote their culture as well as induce tourists to have an in-depth and convenient trip in the village.
