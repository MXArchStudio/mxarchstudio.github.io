From Roof to Table

2025 KTH Thesis project Location:Kungens Kurva IKEA, Huddinge, Stockholm, Sweden
This design project is inspired by the concept of vertical farming. Using the IKEA building in Kungens Kurva as a case study, it explores generic strategies for rooftop urban farming in Stockholm, Sweden. The project identifies the limitations of existing models by examining current urban farming practices in Sweden in terms of their conceptual approach, farming techniques, and financial conditions. From an architectural perspective, the project seeks to utilize underused urban spaces to reduce initial farm costs, shorten food supply chains, and strengthen urban food security. From a functional perspective, it redefines the farm beyond a purely productive space by integrating agricultural spaces into urban life and introducing public, educational, and interactive programs such as farm cafés, workshops, and exhibition spaces for disseminating new agricultural knowledge and techniques. Beyond site selection and programming, as a renovation project, it investigates architectural approaches for introducing new structures onto an expansive existing rooftop while addressing structural constraints as well as the cultural and contextual conditions of the site.
