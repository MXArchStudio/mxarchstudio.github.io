About

Here is Mengxue (Doris)，I am running MX Studio to optimizing design management to save your costs, tailoring spaces to fit your life.

Grounded in 14 years of experience spanning art, design, and architecture, MX Studio was founded on a core belief: architecture is neither a symbol nor a narrative—it is a vessel and a medium. We define spaces through floors, ceilings, and walls to provide comfort and safety, while opening doors and windows to inspire freedom and joy. Beyond individual users, architecture holds a profound social responsibility, impacting industries and the environment alike. Our mission is to deliver responsible, purposeful design that creates lasting value. By optimizing design management to save costs and tailoring spaces to enrich lives, we let exceptional work drive our practice forward.。
