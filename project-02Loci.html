Genius Loci

2022 competition work with Bin Jia&Jingyuan Zheng Location:Nanjing City, Jiangsu Province, China.
This is a competition project which requires us to design three infrastructures for the camping site in the given plot. Because of the tranquil, organic scenario in this park, we decided to follow the natural topographic changes and preserve the landscape of the site as much as possible. The challenge in this concept is how to minimize the attachment between the building and the ground, and suspend the ground floors through cores and tensioned structures. As buildings require to be able to serve camping activities, we scatter them alongside an artless, circular walk path in that to create a private zone around woods whist giving passengers the convenient access into the buildings. Apart from the environmental and functional context, we also hope the buildings could be "easy to build" and "easy to move" by using steel structures, contributing to the sustainable society.
